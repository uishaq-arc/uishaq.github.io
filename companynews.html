Monday, January 3, 2018 - 2014 Annual Year-End Performance Review Process Has Begun

Ventas is transitioning to a new, more robust HR and Payroll system called Workday which will replace Paycor.

Thisintuitive system will make it easier and faster to complete tasks and access information from your computer or mobile device including:

  • View or change personal information
  • Check benefits
  • Enter time
  • Request time off
  • View organizational charts

Training on Workday will be offered in early-June to help prepare you for the common tasks and activities you will complete in the system, with a full transition to Workday in mid-June. HR will keep you posted on key dates as the date approaches to the company-wide launch.

For more information, please click hereto watch a brief overview video of the Workday platform.
